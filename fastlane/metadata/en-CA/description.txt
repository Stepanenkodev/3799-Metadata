Strike Legend is a boxing simulattor roguelike where you control a fighter and fight through the grounds of a tournament series. But it's up to you, if you want try out Arcade mode first. In Arcade Mode, you’ll face off against random opponnents, earning in game coins with each victory. But if you’re in it for the long ride, Career Mode lets you battle throughh nine opponents in a row, with the ultimate goal of becoming the World Champion.

From your fight you will earn in game coins which you can then later spend on upgradind your stats improve your chances on coming to top. The pick is yours, boost your health, precision, power, and more, each upgrade is viable and provides an opportunity for different stratedy. When playing Career mode you have one health bar for the entire run which brings a tough decision do you train to levell up your abilitties or rest to recover health for the next round? And if you lose, you start from 0, but that the beauty of roguelikesFind your strategy, evolve, train and come to the top to become World Champion Boxer
